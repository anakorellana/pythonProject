About Me

Hello, I’m Ana Karina



I grew up in Mexico but have lived between France, the States and Mexico since I was 15. I definitely believe that the experiences that I have lived in those countries and the people I met there have shaped me into who I am today, a catastrophe. One of my talents is that I can make myself laugh until my tummy hurts and if you are funny enough you could have that effect on me too. I always wanted to have a personal blog but never got myself to do it until now that I am pursuing a career in WebDev. Building this blog on my own along with Corey’s help is a lot more fulfilling and pleasant. I wanted to have a place where I could just write my random thoughts while traveling, hiking, running, pursuing a new career, reading a book, listening to an amazing podcast or church talk or anything else I might be obsessing about. I don't know if anyone will read anything from here. But, if somebody does I hope it can bring some light, comfort or enthusiasm into their lives.
